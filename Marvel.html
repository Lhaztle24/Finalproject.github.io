Welcome to the Universe!

Marvel Comics is a renowned American comic book publisher. It is known for creating iconic characters such as Spider-Man, Iron Man, Captain America, the Hulk, Thor, Black Widow, and many more.

About Marvel

Founded in 1939, Marvel has become one of the most influential entertainment brands in the world. In addition to comics, Marvel expanded its presence into various media platforms, including movies, TV shows, video games, and merchandise.

Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU)

The Marvel Cinematic Universe, commonly referred to as the MCU, is a shared universe that brings Marvel's characters to life on the big screen. It has produced a series of interconnected superhero films, generating immense popularity and critical acclaim. The MCU is responsible for creating a cohesive narrative spanning multiple franchises and introducing groundbreaking crossover events.
